Services

Allow patients to schedule appointments with healthcare providers, access medical records, and communicate with healthcare professionals. This can be particularly helpful for patients who want to manage their health care in a more efficient and convenient manner.

Administrative services

These may include patient registration and admission, insurance and billing services, medical records management, and appointment scheduling.

Ancillary services

These may include laboratory and imaging services, pharmacy services, rehabilitation services, and nutrition services.

Emergency services

emergency medical care, trauma care, and critical care services for patients who require immediate attention.

Telemedicine services

These may include remote medical consultations, virtual follow-up visits, and online appointment scheduling and management.

Outpatient services

These may include medical consultations, diagnostic tests, and minor procedures that do not require an overnight stay.

Inpatient services

These may include medical and surgical procedures that require hospitalization and monitoring, such as surgeries, childbirth, and intensive care.
